About Us

Our company specializes in exporting cars from China to various countries around the world. The headquarters is located in Tianjin, China. We successfully deliver cars to countries such as Ukraine, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Moldova, Azerbaijan, and Armenia.

As a reliable car exporter, we have established close partnerships with many leading Chinese automobile companies. We guarantee our clients the supply of original spare parts and components, ensuring high quality and durability

Advantages of cars from China:

  • Wide range of models: Chinese automakers offer a broad selection, from compact city cars to luxurious SUVs.
  • Affordable cost: cars from China are competitively priced, making them an attractive choice for buyers.
  • Advanced technologies: Chinese automakers actively incorporate the latest technologies into their models, including advanced safety systems and smart features.
